## Tumbling Reels: Mekanik Inti

**Tumbling Reels** (atau cascade/avalanche) adalah jantung Gates of Olympus:

### Cara Kerja

1. Spin menghasilkan simbol di grid 6×5
2. Jika 8+ simbol matching (pay anywhere), mereka **explode** dan hilang
3. Simbol baru **jatuh dari atas** mengisi kosong
4. Proses repeat jika tumble baru create win
5. Satu spin bisa produce multiple consecutive wins

Tumbling continues until no new winning combination — all dalam **satu spin paid once** at base bet (tumbles free within spin).

### Implikasi Strategi

- Single spin value >> face value bet — one spin could chain 5+ tumbles
- Multiplier orbs (discussed below) accumulate during tumble sequence
- Patience required — dry spins normal, tumble chains sporadic

### Perbandingan dengan Slot Klasik

Slot payline tradisional: one result per spin, done. Gates: one spin = potential mini-session of tumbles. Volatility feels higher because expectation tied to chain length, not single reel stop.
