## RTP, Volatility, and Complexity — How to Think About All Three

**RTP (Return to Player)** is long-run statistical return. A 99% RTP game still lets you lose your whole session in an hour if variance is high and bet sizing is aggressive.

**Volatility** is how swingy short sessions feel. Crash at 2x targets is different from Crash at 20x targets even on the same RTP.

**Complexity** is cognitive load — not the same as difficulty. Keno has simple clicks but paytable nuance; Mines has simple rules but hard cashout decisions.

Smart players match **volatility to bankroll**, not chase the highest multiplier on the list.
