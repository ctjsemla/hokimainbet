Crash is one of the most popular games on **Stake** and other crypto casinos. The multiplier climbs from 1.00x until a random crash point. Your job is simple: **cash out before it drops**.

## What Is Crash Game?

Each round starts at **1.00x** and rises continuously. If you cash out at **2.50x**, your payout is bet x 2.5. If the game crashes before your cashout, the round is lost.

> Crash outcomes are independent and generated through provably fair logic.

## How to Place a Bet

1. Open Crash lobby (or demo mode in HokiMainbet).
2. Set your stake size.
3. Press **Bet** before countdown ends.
4. Hit **Cash Out** during the round to lock in current multiplier.

## Beginner Strategy

### Conservative multiplier targets

Most beginners overchase 10x+ and burn balance quickly. Repeated targets in the **1.5x-2.2x** range reduce variance and improve session control.

### Use 1-2% bankroll sizing

A simple rule: never risk more than 1-2% of your balance per round.

### Enable auto cashout

Auto cashout helps remove emotional hesitation when charts move too fast.

## Common Mistakes

- Chasing the next round after a huge crash.
- Increasing bet size aggressively after losses.
- Ignoring variance and session limits.

## Final Takeaway

Crash rewards discipline more than prediction. Start small, use realistic targets, and increase risk only after you can follow your own rules consistently.
