## Tumbling Reels: Core Mechanic

**Tumbling Reels** (also called cascade or avalanche) is the heart of Gates of Olympus:

### How It Works

1. Spin produces symbols on the 6×5 grid
2. If 8+ matching symbols (pay anywhere), they **explode** and disappear
3. New symbols **fall from above** to fill gaps
4. Process repeats if new tumble creates a win
5. One spin can produce multiple consecutive wins

Tumbling continues until no new winning combination — all within **one spin paid once** at base bet (tumbles are free within the spin).

### Strategy Implications

- Single spin value exceeds face bet value — one spin could chain 5+ tumbles
- Multiplier orbs (discussed below) accumulate during tumble sequence
- Patience required — dry spins are normal, tumble chains sporadic

### Comparison with Classic Slots

Traditional payline slots: one result per spin, done. Gates: one spin equals potential mini-session of tumbles. Volatility feels higher because expectation ties to chain length, not single reel stop.
